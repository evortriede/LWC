Online Payments

London Water Co-op (LWC) now offers the capability to make online payments with credit or debit cards.
We offer this capability as a convenience to our members and their renters.
The convenience of paying by credit card comes at a cost, which we pass on to you as a "convenience fee" that you can see in the box with that label below. This fee simply covers our cost in offering this feature to you.

Instructions:


Fill in your first and last name, email address, property address and the amount that you wish to pay toward the fees due to LWC.
The convenience fee amount will be displayed as you enter the amount and will be added to the total.
When you press the Pay button, you will be taken to a secure payment page where your credit card information will be collected and the payment will be processed.
